BackRub



Page and Brin met at Stanford University in 1995, where both were doctoral students in the computer science program. In 1996, Larry Page began working on a research project, it was a search engine called “Back Rub” with the help of Sergey Brin with the aim of classifying websites into a group of importance by using their back link data and using it as a criteria to display engine results. This basically means to rank information on the web by the popularity of links, where a page with more backlinks which are links that point from other websites to your website would rank higher.

PageRank Algorithm

  • In 1998, they developed the PageRank algorithm which was the foundation for Google.

  • It’s a system that’s used to rank websites in their search engine results page.

  • It determined the relevance of the website by the number of pages and the importance of pages that linked back to the original website.

  • Their goal was to improve the way computer users search for information on the internet by organizing and presenting the information that is available to them efficiently.

  • This algorithm is what allows Google to find the most relevant web pages quickly.

The Creation of Google

  • The project BackRub was renamed Google.

  • It was inspired from the misspelling of the word googol.

  • To further their search engine, they raised $1 million from investors and close ones, and officially incorporated Google on September 4, 1998.

  • Google has since become the world's most popular search engine, receiving an average of 5.9 billion searches per day in 2013.

  • Google's search engine fundamentally changed how we find and consume information.

  • It brought knowledge to our fingertips, giving access to the vast expanse of the internet, becoming an essential part of our lives.

Further Innovations and Expansions

Alphabet Inc.



Inaddition to its major search engine, Google has developed products like Android, Gmail, Google Docs, Google Drive and Google Cloud, Waymo as well as acquiring companies such as YouTube and Nest. Google and its other subsidiaries, is under the parent company known as Alphabet Inc., which was established in 2015. It has grown significantly through acquiring and developing a variety of other Internet, products, businesses, and services.
